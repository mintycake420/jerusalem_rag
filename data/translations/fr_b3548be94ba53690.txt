**Concerning this Volume**

Be it known that this is a digital likeness of a work preserved for many generations upon the shelves of a library, before being transcribed with great care by the House of Google. This was done as part of a labor to allow the people of all nations to discover the gathered literary heritage of the world by way of the electronic arts.

This book, being of considerable antiquity, is no longer shielded by the laws of privilege—that is to say, copyright—and hath now entered into the common domain. To "belong to the common domain" signifieth that the work in question was never subject to the rights of authorship, or that the legal term of such rights hath expired. The requirements for a book to fall into the common domain may vary from one realm to another. Books free of such legal bonds serve as bridges to the days of yore; they stand as witnesses to the bounty of our history, our cultural inheritance, and the treasury of human wisdom, which are too often hidden from the eyes of the public.

The glosses [marginal notes] and other annotations set within the margins of the original codex are preserved in this file, serving as a remembrance of the long pilgrimage this work hath made, from the house of the stationer through the library, to find itself at last within your hands.
