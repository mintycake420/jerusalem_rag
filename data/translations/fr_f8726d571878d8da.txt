**Scholarly Notes:**

[1] **Assizes of the High Court (Assises de la haute cour):** The *Assises* refer to the body of medieval feudal law compiled in the Latin East. The *Haute Cour* (High Court) was the supreme feudal council of the Kingdom of Jerusalem, consisting of the king and his direct vassals, serving as the highest judicial and political authority.
