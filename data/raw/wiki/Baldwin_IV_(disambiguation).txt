TITLE: Baldwin IV (disambiguation)

Baldwin IV of Jerusalem (1161–1185) was the king of Jerusalem from 1174 until his death in 1185.
Baldwin IV may also refer to:

Baldwin IV, Count of Flanders (980–1035), count of Flanders from 987 to 1035
Baldwin IV, Count of Hainaut (1108–1171), count of Hainaut from 1120 to 1171
Wade Baldwin IV (born 1996), American professional basketball player
